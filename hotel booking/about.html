HOTEL BOOKING


Hotelbooking(www.hb.com) is the World leader in booking hotel and other accommodation online. It guarantees the best prices for any type of property - from small independents to five-star luxury. Guests can access the Hotelbooking(www.hb.com) website anytime, anywhere from their desktops, mobile phones and tablet devices, and they don't pay booking fees - ever. Each day, over 650,000 room nights are reserved on this website.



Hotelbooking(www.hb.com) website is available in 41 languages, offers more than 425,000 hotels and accommodations in 195 countries, features over 25 million reviews written by guests after their stay, and attracts online visitors from both leisure and business markets around the globe. With over 17 years of experience and a team of over 6,500 dedicated employees in 115 + offices worldwide, Hotelbooking(www.hb.com) operates its own in-house customer service team, which is available 24/7 to assist guests in their native languages and ensure an exceptional customer experience.



The idea behind Booking.com is simple - provide an online platform where hotels make their rooms available to prospective guests, and to generate room sales. A user-friendly system provides hotels with the tools to manage their online inventory efficiently to suit their needs.Hotels determine and set the room price in their own extranet; Hotelbooking(www.hb.com) only provides the 'platform', which the hotels upload their availability and relevant room prices to and consumer can directly book a room with the property of his/her choice. Working with connectivity solutions like channel managers or CRS systems provides further options to manage inventory. Booking.com sends the guest's reservations directly to the property and the confirmation back to the guest.
